# AnnaSetu -- Turning Surplus Food into Lifesaving Support

🍽️ AnnaSetu – AI Powered Food Rescue Network<br>

📌 Overview<br>
AnnaSetu is a real-time food rescue platform that connects restaurants with surplus food to nearby NGOs and volunteers. It enables quick reporting, instant alerts, and efficient last-mile delivery, ensuring excess food reaches needy people instead of being wasted<br><br>

<br><br>❗ Problem Statement<br>
Large amounts of food are wasted daily by restaurants and events<br>
Many people still suffer from hunger and lack of food access<br>
Lack of coordination between food donors and NGOs<br>
Delay in food distribution leads to spoilage<br><br>

💡 Solution<br>
AnnaSetu provides a digital platform that enables:<br>
Real-time reporting of surplus food<br>
Instant NGO notification and response<br>
Volunteer-based pickup and delivery<br>
Efficient and fast food redistribution<br><br>

⚙️ How It Works<br>
Restaurant uploads excess food details on the website<br>
System sends instant alerts to nearby NGOs<br>
NGO accepts the request<br>
Volunteer is assigned for pickup<br>
Food is delivered to needy people<br>
Impact is tracked on dashboard<br><br>
