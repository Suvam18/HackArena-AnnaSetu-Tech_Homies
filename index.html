Got Questions?

Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about using AnnaSetu for food rescue, distribution safety, and community eligibility.

What is AnnaSetu exactly?

AnnaSetu is a digital "Food Bridge" designed to connect establishments with surplus food directly to NGOs. Our mission is to eliminate food waste by making donations seamless and safe.

Is there any cost for using the platform?

No — AnnaSetu is entirely free for both donor restaurants and recipient NGOs. Our platform is a non-profit venture aimed at building a sustainable, hunger-free future for all.

How do you ensure food safety?

Safety is our top priority. Both donors and NGOs follow strict local guidelines. Our AI model also assists donors in logging freshness data to ensure all rescued food is wholesome.

Who is eligible to join?

Any verified food establishment (restaurants, hotels, caterers) or registered non-profit organization (NGOs, community kitchens) can join the rescue network today.
